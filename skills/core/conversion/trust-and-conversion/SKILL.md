## Purpose

Good conversion design lowers the effort and risk of saying yes — it does not manipulate.
Trust compounds; tricks convert once and cost the relationship.

## Rules

- One obvious primary path per step; remove competing calls-to-action and needless fields.
- Reduce cognitive load: show progress, defer optional detail, keep the next step clear.
- Make trust visible where doubt lives: clear pricing, security/returns near the action, real social proof, plain terms.
- Reassure at the moment of commitment (what happens next, how to undo).

## Anti-patterns (dark patterns — never use)

Hidden costs, forced continuity, confirmshaming, disguised ads, pre-checked consent, fake scarcity/urgency, roach-motel cancellation. These are prohibited, not tactics.
